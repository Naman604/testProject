The functions of the application are going to help people who are in the need of medical help.
The application will help people to know about the updates on COVID-19 cases in their surrounding. 
The toll and graphs of total number of cases, number of people deceased, number of people recovered from the infection, number of people vaccinated will be one of the main features of the application.  
Pros of Vaccination, Symptoms of COVID-19, Precautions to be taken are also going to be a part of the application. 
Donations to help the needy can also be made through  the app.
